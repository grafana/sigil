# Understand Grafana Sigil pricing

{{< docs/public-preview product="Grafana Sigil" >}}

Grafana Sigil is open-source software. You can self-host Sigil at no licensing cost.

For Grafana Cloud, Sigil pricing details and plan tiers are subject to change during the preview period. Refer to the [Grafana pricing page](https://grafana.com/pricing/) for the latest rates and included usage.

## Self-hosted

Self-hosted Sigil has no licensing fees. You're responsible for infrastructure costs including compute, MySQL, object storage, and the observability stack (Prometheus, Tempo, Alloy).

## Grafana Cloud

During the private preview, Grafana Cloud pricing for Sigil is determined by your agreement with Grafana Labs. Contact your Grafana Labs account team for details.
